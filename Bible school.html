Our Bible School
Training to serve people

Recuruting, training, equiping & sending out Leaders for the great commision.

Bible school is a theological training of our church ministers to take them through the discipline of church and equipping them with skills of how to manage and grow a church progressively.
Giving out certificates after study.
The Bible is meant to be bread for daily use
Preaching the gospel

Mission

After this training, someone is given a certificate in these basic skills of church discipline & as we having a vision of recruiting, equipping and sending out missionaries around the world we are confident that after this training an individual is able to start up a church / ministry and manage it well feeding God’s flock well, and with good motives.
Every year we train 40 – 60 Men and women of God in these Disciplines and graduate them in a bunch to go and serve the world.

Lessons taught in Bible school


  • Church Administration
  • Pastoral Theology
  • Christian Doctrine
  • Sermon preparation and Preaching
  • New Testament Survey
  • Old testament Survey
