  • Every church member is expected to be part of a life group as a subfamily of the whole big family of Ishaka Community Church.
  • The life groups are hosted by church members on a rotation basis depending on place of residence and lead by church ministers trained to mentor and show love to the family life group.
  • The life group families meet once in a week for 1hr and 30 min on Wednesday # mid-week to keep the fire burning in the family.
  • The life groups are used as a form of maintaining a family and showing love to one another through breaking bread together, praying for each other and growing in the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ as a family.
  • As well the life groups work as a tool of Evangelism in the community, and many people have joined church after experiencing the Love of God through the Life groups.
  • The life groups are also a great tool to the ministry, through mentoring and growing passionate ministers who join the ministry on the recommendation of the Life group Leaders.
