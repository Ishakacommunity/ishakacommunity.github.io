Who we are?

ICC YOUTH is more than just another youth service. ICC Youth is a culture of students being built towards their callings, doing life together, and playing a direct role in seeing their lives changed.

We connect with other young adults, building Christ-centered relationships; sharing in each other’s struggles, triumphs, visions, and dreams.

The ICC Youth group families meet once in a week for 1hr and 30 min on Wednesday # mid-week to keep the fire burning in the family. and from there we go out together, pushing one another to be all that God has designed us to be.

ICC Youth  is more than something you attend, it's a community that becomes a part of you.
